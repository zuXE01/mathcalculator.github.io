What is cubed in math?

An easy way to write 3 cubed is 3^3. This means three multiplied by itself three times. The easiest way to do this calculation is to do the first multiplication (3x3) and then to multiply your answer by the same number you started with; 3 x 3 x 3 = 9 x 3 = 27.
